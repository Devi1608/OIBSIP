JAWAHARLAL NEHRU

Most influential freedom fighter of India.

  • Born - 14 November 1889
  • Died - 27 May 1964
  • Occupation - Indian anti-colonial nationalist, secular humanist, social democrat, statesman and author
  • About - Jawaharlal Nehru was an Indian anti-colonial nationalist, secular humanist, social democrat, statesman and author who was a central figure in India during the middle of the 20th century. Nehru was a principal leader of the Indian nationalist movement in the 1930s and 1940s
  • Title - The Greatest Freedom Fighter of India
  • Achivements - Internationalising the struggle for Indian independence: 1927. Nehru played a leading role in the development of the internationalist outlook of the Indian independence struggle.
  • Spouse - Kamala Kaul
